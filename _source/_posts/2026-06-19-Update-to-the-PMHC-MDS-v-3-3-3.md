#### PMHC MDS Update v3.3.3 ####

We are pleased to announce that PMHC MDS Update v3.3.3 was released on 
June 19, 2026.

The following updates have been applied in the reporting interface:
* Fixed a bug where the M5 and M6 reports returned a blank screen

The following updates have been applied to the upload and data entry interfaces:
* Fixed a bug where Organisation Type referred to at Intake Conclusion with a 
  value of ‘44: HeadtoHelp / HeadtoHealth’ or ‘42: AMHC’ was being prevented from 
  being retained on existing data.

A bug was also fixed that prevented some nightly generated emails from being sent.

The user guides have been updated to reflect these updates. They are available
at [https://docs.pmhc-mds.com/](https://docs.pmhc-mds.com/){:target="_blank"}.
