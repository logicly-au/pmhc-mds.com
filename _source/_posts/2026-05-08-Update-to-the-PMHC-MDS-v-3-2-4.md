#### PMHC MDS Update v3.2.4 ####

We are pleased to announce that PMHC MDS Update v3.2.4 was released on 
May 8, 2026.

The following updates have been applied in the upload interface:
* Fixed a bug where emails were not being sent after a successful upload.
* Improved the error returned when the episode_tags column is missing in 
  an episodes worksheet/file. 

The following updates have been applied in the login interface:
* Fixed a bug where new users couldn’t log in after accepting their invite.


The user guides have been updated to reflect these updates. They are available
at [https://docs.pmhc-mds.com/](https://docs.pmhc-mds.com/){:target="_blank"}.
