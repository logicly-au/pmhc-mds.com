#### PMHC MDS Update v3.2.5 ####

We are pleased to announce that PMHC MDS Update v3.2.5 was released on 
May 15, 2026.

The following updates have been applied in the user management interface:
* User search and listing of users such as in the user audit emails, now 
  excludes users who have no roles at relevant organisations. 

The following updates have been applied in the login interface:
* Fixed a bug where roles were not being assigned to new users.

The user guides have been updated to reflect these updates. They are available
at [https://docs.pmhc-mds.com/](https://docs.pmhc-mds.com/){:target="_blank"}.
